Oven Instructions


  1. Preheat oven to 425¬∞F

  2. Cut the chicken into bite sized pieces

  3. Whisk the eggs in a bowl

  4. Cover the chicken in the egg

  5. Mix some flour and italian seasoning in a bowl

  6. Now Place the chicken into the flour mixture, make sure it's thinly coated

  7. Put the chicken on a pan with aluminum foil (Note: we did not it and it was a pain)

  8. Put the pan in the oven for 20 minutes flipping it half way through

  9. Take the chicken out of the oven and dunk it in sauce until coated evenly and place the pan back in the oven for 5 minutes

  10. Take the chicken out of the oven, drizzle a second layer of sauce, and then put it back in the oven for 2-3 minutes

Stovetop Instructions


  1. Cut the chicken into bite sized pieces

  2. Whisk the eggs in a bowl

  3. Cover the chicken in the egg

  4. Mix some flour and italian seasoning in a bowl

  5. Now Place the chicken into the flour mixture, make sure it's thickly coated

  6. Add enough vegetable oil to a large skillet to reach about 1 inch deep. Heat the oil over medium-high heat until it reaches 375¬∞F

  7. Carefully place the breaded chicken pieces into the hot oil in a single layer

  8. Avoid overcrowding the pan. Fry until golden brown and cooked through to an internal temperature of 165¬∞F

  9. Use tongs to remove the cooked chicken from the oil. Place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess grease.

  10. In a large bowl, toss the hot, fried chicken pieces with your favorite wing sauce until they are evenly coated.
